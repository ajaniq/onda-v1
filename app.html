ONDA

Tag your tracks (genre, energy, key, rating, notes…), preview them with a player, and build genre-sorted USB crates for your sets.

ONDA reads your music and saves tags into the files — it never deletes your music. Sending to a USB copies by default, so your originals stay safe (you can switch to Move anytime).

FAQ
Does it harm files?No deletions. It writes tags into files; "Move" relocates them, but USB defaults to Copy so originals stay.
Where are tags saved?In each file, plus ~/Library/Application Support/ONDA — so updates never lose your library.

Clean up genres

Pre-checked (auto) = real spelling duplicates: differences in spacing, punctuation, (notes), or known abbreviations — e.g. JerseyClub=Jersey Club, SPEED GARAGE (ISH)=Speed Garage, DnB=Drum & Bass.
Unchecked (your OK needed) = capitalization-only changes (WORLD→World, HOUSE→House) and look-alikes. Sub-genres are never merged into parents. Edit the “→” name for any group.
